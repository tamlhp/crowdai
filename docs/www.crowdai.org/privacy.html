Types and Use of Data collected

crowdAI collects personal data such as your name, e-mail address, affiliation and other information you give when creating an account on crowdAI as a user of crowdAI website or as an organizer of a challenge. For operation and maintenance purposes, crowdAI.org may collect files that record interaction with crowdAI website (System Logs) or use for this purpose other personal data such as your IP Address.

crowdAI only uses your personal data for the operation of crowdAI website and services, including the challenges, as well as to improve crowdAI website and services.

Other personal data may be collected on crowdAI website along dedicated explanation text contextually with any such data collection.

If you provide personal data to any Organizer of a challenge on crowdAI, this will be according to each organizer’s data protection/privacy policy. CrowdAI does not take responsibility for such personal data.

crowdAI may use your contact data to send you information about crowdAI challenges and crowdAI.org other services.

Mode and place of processing the personal data

Methods of processing

crowdAI processes the user’s and organizer’s personal data in a proper manner and shall take appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or unauthorized destruction of the data.

The personal data is processed by crowdAI at its operating offices in Switzerland. The personal data processed by crowdAI is kept as long as you or crowdAI has not cancelled your account though crowdAI may keep the data after cancellation of your account as long as necessary for technical reasons (like backups) or to comply with legal requirements. Challenge submission data is retained for research purposes, for up to 10 years.
